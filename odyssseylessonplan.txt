Odyssey Lesson Plan

1. Introduction to Money and the History of Currency
 • What is money?
 • Evolution from barter to digital currencies
 • The problem with fiat currencies and central banking

2. What Is Bitcoin?
 • Overview of Bitcoin as a digital currency
 • Who created Bitcoin? (Satoshi Nakamoto)
 • Bitcoin's goals: decentralization, limited supply, censorship resistance

3. How Bitcoin Works
 • Blockchain basics
 • What is mining?
 • Nodes and the peer-to-peer network
 • Public/private key cryptography

4. Bitcoin vs Traditional Money
 • Inflation and monetary policy
 • Limited supply of Bitcoin (21 million cap)
 • Trustless system vs centralized institutions

5. Getting Started with Bitcoin
 • How to buy Bitcoin
 • Wallet types: custodial vs non-custodial
 • How to send/receive Bitcoin safely

6. Bitcoin Security
 • Best practices for securing your Bitcoin
 • Private key management and seed phrases
 • Common scams and how to avoid them

7. Bitcoin Use Cases
 • Store of value (digital gold)
 • Remittances and cross-border payments
 • Micropayments and financial sovereignty

8. The Bitcoin Ecosystem
 • Exchanges, wallets, payment processors
 • Bitcoin Lightning Network (brief intro)
 • Mining industry and energy debate

9. Risks, Challenges, and Criticisms
 • Price volatility
 • Regulatory uncertainty
 • Environmental concerns
 • Misconceptions and myths

10. The Future of Bitcoin
 • Potential for mass adoption
 • Bitcoin's role in global finance
 • Ongoing development and innovations
